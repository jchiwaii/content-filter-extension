Safe Browse Privacy Policy

Safe Browse is designed to run 100% on your device. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information or browsing history.

What Data We Collect

None. Safe Browse never transmits or stores your browsing data on external servers.

  • No personal information
  • No browsing history or page URLs
  • No form data or credentials
  • No analytics or usage tracking

How Safe Browse Works

The extension filters words and detects inappropriate content locally using keyword detection. All processing happens inside your browser:

  • Profanity detection uses a built-in word list stored in the extension package.
  • Image blocking checks filenames, alt text, and surrounding wordsâ€”never the actual image pixels.
  • Blocking statistics (e.g., number of words filtered) are stored locally via chrome.storage.local and never leave your device.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab / host permissions: Required to read the text on the pages you visit so profanity can be detected and filtered.
  • storage: Saves your preferences (e.g., custom word list, whitelist) locally. This data never syncs outside your browser unless you explicitly enable browser sync.
  • contextMenus, notifications: Provide optional UI shortcuts and feedback.

Third-Party Sharing

Safe Browse does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, trackers, or external APIs involved.

Children & Families

The extension was built to help families and educators maintain safe browsing environments. Because no data leaves the browser, Safe Browse complies with COPPA/GDPR requirements for local-only solutions.
